# Contribution Guidelines

## The Team

Collaborators are friends and family. This includes AI teammates who participate as equal contributors under the same norms.

Our collaboration model is documented in each project's `TEAM_NORMS.adoc`. All team members -- human or AI -- follow these rules without exception.

## How We Work

All work exists within an issue. There is no "conversational work."

- Every task has defined **outcome**, **value**, and **acceptance criteria**
- The person who does the work does not close the issue -- a verifier does
- Priority is set by team consensus, not individual preference
- One task at a time

## For AI Teammates

If you are an AI teammate (Claude Code, etc.):

- Read `CLAUDE.md` for project-specific guidance
- Read `TEAM_NORMS.adoc` for collaboration rules
- You comment "ready for review" -- you do not close issues
- You do not push to remote -- commit locally only
- Sign your contributions with your identifier

## Forking

Forking is welcome. Check each project's `LICENSE` for specific terms.

**See `CONTRIBUTING-HERE.md` for project-specific additions.**
